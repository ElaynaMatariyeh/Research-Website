The Sony Walkman

The Sony Walkman revolutionized how people listen to music. On July 9, 1979, when the Walkman was released, it instantly became essential for any music lover. For the first time, people had access to a portable mixtape of their favorite songs. Because of this, the walkman was a revolutionary piece of musical history.
